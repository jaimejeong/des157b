The Davis Centennial Seal features stories about the Davis community and symbolizes its history and development throughout the centuries by exploring themes that connect us to our past, including stories of marginalized communities.

One of the panels illustrates the Thong Hy Huynh Award, an award to raise awareness and honor Thong Hy Huynh, a Davis High School student who was stabbed to death in a racially motivated attack on May 4, 1983.
